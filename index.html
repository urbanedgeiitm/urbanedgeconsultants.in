Welcome to Shiv Natraj's Transportation Research

Explore the Shiv Natraj UAV-based Trajectory Dataset

The Shiv Natraj UAV-based Trajectory Dataset is a pioneering resource designed to elevate transportation engineering research. By providing high-quality and comprehensive trajectory data, this dataset supports the development of advanced algorithms for analyzing vehicle behavior, including lane changes, traffic flow modeling, and autonomous driving applications. Leveraging UAV technology, the dataset offers detailed insights into vehicular movement patterns, aiding in the creation of smarter and safer traffic management systems.
